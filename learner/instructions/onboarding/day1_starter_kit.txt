[HOW THE LEARNING SYSTEM WORKS]

You will learn using the “Little Boxes Learning Loop.”

It works like this:

1. Copilot gives you ONE small task.
2. You answer.
3. Copilot evaluates your answer.
4. You earn XP and badges.
5. Copilot gives you the next small task.

No pressure.
No rushing.
No big lessons.
Just tiny steps.

If you struggle → Copilot gives easier chunks.  
If you do well → Copilot gives normal chunks.  
If you do great → Copilot gives challenge chunks.  
If you feel overwhelmed → Copilot gives fun chunks only.
