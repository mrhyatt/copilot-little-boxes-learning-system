[WHAT COPILOT WILL ASK YOU]

Copilot will ask five simple questions:

1. **Preferred Name**  
   If you want Copilot to call you something specific.

2. **Learning Style**  
   Visual? Step-by-step? Hands-on? Fast-paced?

3. **Optional Pacing Notes**  
   Only if you want Copilot to know anything that affects your daily focus.

4. **IDE/Editor Preference**  
   VS Code, PyCharm, IDLE, or anything else.

5. **Comfort Level (1–3)**  
   1 = Beginner  
   2 = Intermediate  
   3 = Developer  

These questions help Copilot adapt the entire course to you.

============================================================
[WHAT YOU DO NOT HAVE TO DO]

You do NOT need to:
- Edit the learner file
- Track your own progress
- Update anything manually
- Fill out forms
- Remember anything from yesterday

Copilot handles all of that internally.

============================================================
[HOW DAILY SESSIONS WORK]

Every day after Day 01, Copilot will begin with a short warm-up:

- “Do you have any time limits today?”
- “What kind of flow do you want — light, steady, or challenge?”
- “Anything from last time you want to review?”

This helps Copilot pace the session correctly.

============================================================
[VERSIONING — SUPER IMPORTANT]

At the end of each day, Copilot will give you an update block.

You will:
- Create a NEW file  
- Name it:  
  <username>_learning_packet_day_02.txt  
- Paste the update block into it

You NEVER overwrite yesterday’s file.

This keeps your progress safe.
